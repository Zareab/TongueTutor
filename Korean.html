한국어 초급 퀴즈 - hangug-eo chogeub kwijeu (Korean Beginner Level Quiz)

1. "eat"의 과거형은 무엇입니까? - "eat"ui gwageohyeong-eun mueos-ibnikka? (What is the past tense of "eat"?)




2. "hot"의 반대말은 무엇입니까? - "hot"ui bandaemal-eun mueos-ibnikka? (Which word means the opposite of "hot"?)




3. "cat"의 복수형은 무엇입니까? - "cat"ui bogsuhyeong-eun mueos-ibnikka? (What is the plural form of "cat"?)



4. "big"의 반대말은 무엇입니까? - "big"ui bandaemal-eun mueos-ibnikka? (Which word means the opposite of "big"?)




5. "happiness"의 유의어는 무엇입니까? - "happiness"ui yuuieoneun mueos-ibnikka? (Which word is a synonym for "happiness"?)




6. "write"의 현재형은 무엇입니까? - "write"ui hyeonjaehyeong-eun mueos-ibnikka? (What is the present tense of "write"?)
