日本語初級クイズ - Nihongo shokyū kuizu (Japanese Beginner Level Quiz)

1. "eat"の過去形は何ですか？- " Eat" no kako katachi wa nanidesu ka? (What is the past tense of "eat"?)




2. "cold"の反対語は何ですか？- " Cold" no hantaikotoba wa nanidesu ka? (Which word means the opposite of "cold"?)




3. "cat"の複数形は何ですか？- " Katto" no fukusūgata wa nanidesu ka? (What is the plural form of "cat"?)



4. "write"の現在形は何ですか？ - " Raito" no genzai katachi wa nanidesu ka? (What is the present tense of "write"?)



5. "happiness"の類義語は何ですか？ - " Happiness" no ruigigo wa nanidesu ka? (Which word is a synonym for "happiness"?)




6. "big"の反対語は何ですか？ - " Big" no hantaikotoba wa nanidesu ka? (Which word means the opposite of "big"?)
